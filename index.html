How it Works

How it works!

Athare revolves around lessons - instructors teach and golfers learn. The golfer enrolls, pays and finishes the lesson - then, the golfer reviews the instructor. This creates a powerful cycle of connecting more golfers with more instructors - the golfer has a trustable, review system to find instructors and instructors who deliver results attract more golfers.

Lesson Creation

Instructors create lessons that teach aspects of the game and golfers enroll and pay for the lesson.

Progress

Golfers learn and practice the specifics of the lesson and instructors grade progress

Completion

Golfers finishes lesson with a new skill and leave feedback for the instructor.

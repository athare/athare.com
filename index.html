How it works

Our coaches can teach through videos, group lessons, or one-on-one classes wherever they are most comfortable. in lessons that people want from the most comfortable spot.

01.

Teach your way

Here at Athare, we know that each instructor has his or her different style. Some coaches prefer videos with little personal instruction while others want as much interaction as possible. Athare gives you the flexibility to create the courses you want in the way that you want.

02.

Earn what you want

Even though teaching can be its own reward, your time and expertise is valuable. Earn income by setting the price for the content that you create.

03.

Grow your student base

If you already have students, you may want more. Athare can build the number of students to the size that you choose by pairing interested students with the coaches that fit their learning style the best.

04.

Instruct from any location

Athare wants to bring your lessons to people around the world so we are designing a platform that makes it easier to teach digitally from anywhere. Teach remote courses from your home, your favorite driving range, or even on your favorite course.

05.

Grow your reputation

By giving the instruction to the students that want to learn from you and getting good reviews, teaching on Athare can give you the boost that you need to increase your reputation for coaching. That means that you can spend less time on the business-side of being a coach and more on actual instruction.

06.

Rest easy

Everything you need to do is handled by Athare so you can focus on being a coach. All you need to do is use our class creator to easily create lessons, classes, videos, and/or schedule time slots to talk with your students so you can focus on your instruction.

About us

Why we created Athare

As avid golfers, we are constantly trying to better our game. Unfortunately it is extremely hard to find people that can teach you the way that you want to be taught and at times that are convenient. When we thought about how we would solve the problem, Athare was born. An online, easy-to-use, effective way of creating course and delivering them to avid golf enthusiasts is the answer to our prayers. Our dream is to have a wide array of knowledge and instructors easily at every student's finger tips and to bring willing and capable students to the coaches that we work with.
